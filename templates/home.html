About Us

Your will is your voice when silence begins, Keep it safe

DigitalWill is a modern and secure platform designed to help individuals create, store, verify, and distribute their last will documents with complete transparency. Using advanced encryption and tamper-proof hashing technology, we ensure that every digital will remains authentic, safe, and accessible only to authorized beneficiaries. Our goal is to simplify the will-handling process while maintaining the highest standards of privacy and legal reliability.

Your Legacy, Protected Digitally.

DigitalWill ensures secure creation, encryption, and verification of will documents, allowing testators and beneficiaries to manage their legacy without fear of unauthorized access or tampering.

End-to-End Encryption

Your will is protected using advanced end-to-end encryption, ensuring that no one—not even the system administrators—can access the content without the correct decryption key. Each document is encrypted individually with a unique key that only the designated beneficiary receives. This guarantees complete privacy and prevents unauthorized access at every stage, from upload to final retrieval.

Automatic Beneficiary Notifications

Whenever a testator uploads or updates their will, the beneficiary receives an instant notification, keeping them fully informed and prepared. These notifications include essential instructions on how to securely access or decrypt the document when the time comes. This ensures a smooth and transparent process, especially during sensitive moments when quick access matters the most.

Multi-Layer Hash Verification

To protect your will from tampering or manipulation, every document undergoes multi-layer hashing using SHA-256 technology. This produces a unique digital fingerprint that cannot be forged or altered. Any future upload of the same document can be compared against the stored hash, quickly revealing whether the file is genuine or has been modified in any way. This ensures complete document authenticity across the system.
