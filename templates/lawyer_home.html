About

This platform enables authorized legal professionals to review, authenticate, and verify the digital wills submitted by registered testators.
The system ensures a secure, structured, and legally compliant workflow for document validation.



Lawyers can access uploaded files, check integrity reports, verify signatures, and provide approvals or clarification.
Our goal is to streamline the legal verification process while maintaining the highest standards of security and confidentiality
